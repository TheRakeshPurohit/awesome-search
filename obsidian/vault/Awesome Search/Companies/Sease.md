# Sease

## About

Italian search engineering and consulting company specializing in Apache Solr, Elasticsearch, and neural information retrieval. Provides training, open-source contributions, and enterprise search consulting. Author and maintainer of [[Rated Ranking Evaluator]] (RRE), a widely-used open-source search-quality evaluation library.

## Focus Areas

- Apache Solr engineering and extensions
- Learning to Rank implementations
- Neural reranking (ColBERT, cross-encoders)
- Search quality evaluation

## Products

- **[[Rated Ranking Evaluator]] (RRE)** — open-source, CI/CD-oriented offline search-quality evaluation library for Solr and Elasticsearch; also offered as the commercial **RRE Enterprise** with a full UI
