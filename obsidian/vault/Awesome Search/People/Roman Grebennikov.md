# Roman Grebennikov

Engineer and co-creator/maintainer of [[Metarank]], the open-source [[Learning to Rank|LTR]] secondary re-ranker. Writes and speaks on practical ranking, [[LambdaMART]], and feature engineering for search relevance. Earlier worked at Findify (white-label e-commerce search), where he built real-time personalized ranking across ~1,500 stores — the experience behind his [[MICES]] 2019 talk.
