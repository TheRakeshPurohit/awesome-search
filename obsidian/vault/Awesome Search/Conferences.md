# Conferences

Search, IR, and e-commerce conferences with dedicated notes in this vault — the recurring venues where the practitioner community gathers and publishes. For the fuller landscape (academic venues, vendor events, meetups) see the [[Events and Conferences]] topic.

- [[Haystack US]] — [[OpenSource Connections]]' search *relevance* conference; the original US edition (Charlottesville, VA). Practitioner-focused on ranking, [[Learning to Rank]], evaluation, and semantic search.
- [[Haystack EU]] — the European edition of Haystack, held in Berlin; overlaps with the other Berlin search events.
- [[Berlin Buzzwords]] — open-source data, search, and scalability conference (Berlin); strong search-engineering track. Home of [[Lester Solbakken]]'s [[Hybrid search > sum of its parts? Berlin Buzzwords 2022|hybrid search benchmarking talk]].
- [[MICES]] (Mix-Camp E-Commerce Search) — dedicated e-commerce search conference (Berlin), organized by [[Rene Kriegler]]; co-located with Berlin Buzzwords.
