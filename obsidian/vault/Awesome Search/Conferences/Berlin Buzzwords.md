# Berlin Buzzwords

**Berlin Buzzwords** is an open-source conference on data storage, search, and scalability, held annually in Berlin. It has a strong search-engineering track and is one of the main European gathering points for the Lucene/Solr/Elasticsearch/OpenSearch community. The e-commerce-focused [[MICES]] conference is co-located with it.

🔗 https://berlinbuzzwords.de/
