# Haystack US

**Haystack** is the search relevance conference run by [[OpenSource Connections]] — the original US edition, held in Charlottesville, Virginia (OSC's home). It is the field's premier practitioner-focused gathering on search *relevance*: ranking, [[Learning to Rank]], evaluation, vector/semantic search, and query understanding across Solr, Elasticsearch, and OpenSearch. The European edition is [[Haystack EU]].

🔗 https://haystackconf.com/

---

## Overview

- **Focus**: search relevance and ranking quality — practitioner talks and workshops, not vendor marketing.
- **Community**: [[Doug Turnbull]], [[Charlie Hull]], and the broader OSC-run [[OpenSource Connections|Relevance Slack]] community are closely associated with it.
- **Related event**: OSC also runs the **Haystack Live** online meetup between conferences (see [[Max Irwin - The Search Engine Migration Circus]], a Haystack Live talk).
