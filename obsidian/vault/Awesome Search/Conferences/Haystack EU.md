# Haystack EU

**Haystack EU** is the European edition of the [[Haystack US|Haystack]] search relevance conference run by [[OpenSource Connections]], held in Europe (Berlin). Like its US counterpart it is practitioner-focused on search *relevance* — ranking, [[Learning to Rank]], evaluation, and semantic/vector search across Solr, Elasticsearch, and OpenSearch. [[Charlie Hull]] is closely associated with the European search-conference scene.

🔗 https://haystackconf.com/

---

## Overview

- **Focus**: same relevance-first, practitioner agenda as [[Haystack US]], for the European community.
- **Community**: overlaps heavily with [[Berlin Buzzwords]] and [[MICES]] (also Berlin-based), and the OSC-run [[OpenSource Connections|Relevance Slack]].
